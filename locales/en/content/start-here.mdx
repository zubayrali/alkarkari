This notebook gathers the teaching of the **Tariqa Karkariya** — a living
Sufi path of the Shadhili line, seated in Morocco under Shaykh Mohamed Faouzi
al-Karkari — for English-speaking seekers. The terms are Arabic; the pages
explain them as they are lived, not merely defined. The [home page](/) is the
doorway; this page is the index you return to.

## Begin

* **[Dictionary](/dictionary)** — the core terms of the Tariqa. Read first,
  return often: [Wird](/dictionary/wird), [Zikr](/dictionary/zikr),
  [Hadra](/dictionary/hadra), [Khalwa](/dictionary/khalwa),
  [Sirr](/dictionary/sirr), and more.
* **[Foundations](/foundations)** — the seven pillars of the path, each with
  its own page.

## Study

  from the Prophet ﷺ through Ibn Mashish, al-Shadhili, and al-Alawi.
